Panettone

Ingridients

 1 package active east
 250 ml warm milk
 1/2 cup of sugar
 3 eggs
 1/2 cup natural yogurt
 1 tsp vanilla extract
 1 tbs greated lemon zest
 1/4 tsp salt
 500 g flour
 1/4 cup dried currants
 1/4 cup raisins
 1/4 cup candied orange peel

Directions
 
 1. In a medium bowl, combine east, milk, sugar. Add eggs, yogurt, vanilla
 	lemon zest, and salt. Mix well. Stir in flour and need until dough in soft
 	and pliable. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour.

 2. In a small bowl toss dried fruits, punch down dough, transfer to floured
 	surace, and need in the fruit.

 3. Place dough in prepared cake pan, clover and let rise for 30 min.
 	Bake in 350 degrees F for 45-50 min.
